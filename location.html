Directions

Just off St Stephen’s Green, there’s a small, low-key shop called PixelForge, a fictional gaming store focused mainly on consoles and everyday accessories. The shop keeps things simple, stocking popular items like controllers, headsets, charging docks, and a selection of pre-owned games for systems like PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X. It also offers basic services like controller repairs and trade-ins, making it a convenient stop for local gamers. The atmosphere is straightforward and practical, with a small display area to test controllers or browse new releases, giving it the feel of a reliable neighbourhood shop rather than anything flashy or over-the-top.
